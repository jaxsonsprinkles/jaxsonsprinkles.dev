About

For the past 0.0000000 years I've been coding my way from training neural nets from scratch to shipping full-stack applications that hundreds of people actually use.

Lately I'm obsessed with new ways of talking to AI - not just chat windows, but places like iMessage, where it can just live in the conversation instead of being a separate app you have to think to open. I like poking at the edges of what AI can do and where it's still clunky, then figuring out how to push past that.

Outside of coding, I'm a varsity athlete competing in cross country and track. I'm fluent in English, Spanish, and Japanese. And I'm probably the world's biggest Chipotle enthusiast.
